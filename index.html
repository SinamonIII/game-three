Game 3

  • High Concept: Run around the map and collect daggers until you are strong enough to kill your enemy.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should enjoy exploring the world.
  • Genre: RPG.
  • Game play: Players can move with the arrow keys, collect items by walking into them, and attempt to kill enemies by walking into them.
  • Prototype goal: This prototype is just to test out exploration of a tile based world.
  • Setting/Premise: This game takes place in a world in which the main character must protect from his enemy.
  • Target Audience: This game is targeted at anyone who enjoys RPGs.
  • Play time: The part of the game that has actually been created doesn't takes around 30 seconds.
  • Strategies: I expect collecting the daggers in their natural order and running into the enemy to kill him will be the only strategy.
